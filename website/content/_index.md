I am a Computer Science PhD student at the [University of Illinois Chicago](https://uic.edu) working on inference-time methods for zero-shot abstractive document summarization with [Professor Cornelia Caragea](https://www.cs.uic.edu/~cornelia/). I completed my master's at [Johns Hopkins](https://jhu.edu), where I was involved in NLP research with [Professor Benjamin van Durme](https://www.cs.jhu.edu/~vandurme/) at the [Center for Language and Speech Processing](https://clsp.jhu.edu). I also did NLP research during my undergrad at [UMass Amherst](https://umass.edu) under the supervision of [Professor Andrew McCallum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andrew_McCallum). Additionally, I have done research internships at [Amazon Alexa AI](https://www.amazon.science/tag/alexa) and at the [University of Southern California](https://usc.edu).
